Leonardo Kanashiro Felizardo

Holds a degree in Civil Engineering from the Polytechnic School of the University of São Paulo (USP) and was an undergraduate researcher in a FINEP project. Holds a Master's degree in Economics from Fundação Getúlio Vargas (FGV), focusing on the topological optimization of artificial neural networks for financial asset price forecasting.

Previously worked as an analyst at the Itaú-Unibanco Group and as a Product Manager at Itaú BBA. Holds a Ph.D. in Computer Engineering from the University of São Paulo (Escola Politécnica) and a Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics from the University of Turin and the Polytechnic University of Turin.

Research interests include Reinforcement Learning applied to financial decision-making processes (automated trading, portfolio optimization, and option pricing and execution) and Operations Research (production and logistics optimization). Currently works as a Data Scientist and Postdoctoral Researcher at the Aeronautics Institute of Technology (ITA), focusing on Genetic Programming, Metaheuristics, and Hyper-Heuristics for optimization problems.
